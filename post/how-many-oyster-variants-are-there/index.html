How many variants of Oyster cards are there?

2023-06-03

You probably have seen many different variants of Oyster cards and wondered how many of them are there in total, and this post is going to answer that by putting together list of them.

Edit: Unfortunately, after putting together the list I still cannot give a precise answer as I am now fairly certain that this list is NOT exhaustive.

Note that only Oyster cards will be covered, which does not include other cards acceptable by Oyster readers (e.g. contactless card, Freedom Pass, ITSO smartcards, …).

All pictures of photocards were responsibly sourced from official examples.

Prior Arts

  • frozenpandaman@ has put together an index of Oyster card design at https://transitcards.xyz/oyster/.
  • Oyster card on Wikipedia has a section listing the variants.
